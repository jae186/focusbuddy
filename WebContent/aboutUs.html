    About Us Page


    Bringing students together to raise grades and lower stress


    It's hard to concentrate, especially if you cannot study at home and have to live in The Commons! But, then you realise that it's depressing to study by yourself, and when you do try to study with friends, you never get any work done! Introducing FocusBuddy, to ensure students have a medium to hold themselves accountable and improve their studying experience by matching them with a buddy who isn't a friend or a complete stranger.
